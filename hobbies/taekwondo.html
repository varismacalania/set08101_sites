Taekwon-do

Synopsis Of The MOST FUN MARTIAL ART!!!

Taekwondo is a Korean martial art focusing on their kicking prowess with the majority of kicks being head height. There are 5 tenets which govern taekwondo. The instructor will prompt the class to repeat this.

Student Oath:

  1. Courtesy
  2. Integrity
  3. Perserverance
  4. Self Control
  5. Indomitable Spirit
